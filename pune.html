PUNE

Pune ,previously known as Poona, (the official name from 1818 until 1978) is a city in Maharashtra state in Deccan plateau in Western India. It is the administrative headquarters of the Pune district, and of Pune division. The city of Pune is part of Pune Metropolitan Region.Pune is one of the largest IT hubs in India.It is also one of the most important automobile and manufacturing hubs of India.Pune is often referred to as "Oxford of the East" because of highly regarded educational institutions in the city.It has been ranked "the most liveable city in India" several times.
