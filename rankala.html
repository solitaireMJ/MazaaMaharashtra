Rankala Lake

Rankala Lake is a picturesque and prominent freshwater lake located in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, India. Spread over an area of approximately 52 acres, the lake serves as a popular recreational spot and a significant landmark for both locals and tourists alike. It was originally constructed by the Chhatrapati rulers of Kolhapur in the 19th century, primarily to meet the city's water needs and to enhance its aesthetics. The lake's serene and tranquil ambiance, coupled with the lush greenery surrounding it, makes it an ideal place for leisurely walks, picnics, and boating activities.
