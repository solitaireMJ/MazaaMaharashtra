Upvan Lake

Upvan Lake is a picturesque freshwater lake located in the city of Thane, Maharashtra, India. Nestled amidst lush greenery and scenic surroundings, this serene lake is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike. The name "Upvan" translates to "upper forest," which aptly describes the natural beauty that surrounds the lake. The lake is man-made and was created by the Thane Municipal Corporation with the purpose of providing a recreational space for the residents of Thane. It spans a considerable area and serves as a tranquil oasis amidst the bustling city life. One of the major attractions of Upvan Lake is its well-maintained promenade or "Upvan Lake Vihar," which offers a pleasant place for people to take leisurely strolls, jog, or simply sit and relax. The promenade is adorned with lush trees and flowering plants, making it an ideal spot for nature enthusiasts and photographers.
