Karnala Bird
Sanctuary

The Karnala Bird Sanctuary, situated in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India, is a popular nature reserve known for its rich avian diversity and stunning landscapes. Spanning an area of approximately 12.11 square kilometers, this sanctuary is a haven for birdwatchers, offering sightings of various resident and migratory bird species. In addition to its avifauna, the sanctuary is also home to the ancient Karnala Fort, attracting history enthusiasts and trekkers. The relatively easy trek to the fort provides breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes. Visitors can enjoy the sanctuary throughout the year, but it's essential to adhere to any guidelines or regulations set by the authorities to preserve the natural environment and ensure the welfare of the wildlife.
