Alibaugh Beach

Alibaug is a town in the Raigad district of Maharashtra. The Alibaug beach is the main beach in the town which is quite a popular beach for planning beach holidays in Maharashtra. The beach here is very neat and tidy and ideal for a long walk along the seaside.

The sandy beach with coconut trees lined up enhances the beauty of the place. The place is not only perfect for leisure holidays but also ideal for planning honeymoons. The sound of the waves, the clear blue sky and the cool breeze blowing in the beach creates a romantic evening, perfect for honeymoon couples.

There are several tourism places in and around the beach which are often visited by tourists. These include the beautiful Kolaba Fort which was built by Shivaji.
