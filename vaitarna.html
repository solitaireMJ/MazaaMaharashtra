Vaitarna Lake

Vaitarna Lake is a picturesque reservoir situated in the state of Maharashtra, India. Nestled amidst the verdant Western Ghats, this man-made lake is a popular tourist destination and a vital source of water supply for the surrounding regions. The lake is formed by the Vaitarna River, which flows through the rugged terrain of the Sahyadri mountain range. The serene and tranquil setting of Vaitarna Lake attracts nature enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and photographers alike. Visitors can revel in the breathtaking views of the lush hills and the crystal-clear waters that reflect the beauty of the landscape. The surroundings of the lake offer ample opportunities for hiking, bird-watching, and enjoying leisurely picnics amidst nature's splendor.
