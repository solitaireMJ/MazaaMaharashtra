Bhimashankar
Wildlife Sanctuary

Bhimashankar Wildlife Sanctuary is situated in the Sahyadri Hills of Western Ghats and is one of the most popular tourist attractions for residents of Pune and Mumbai. The sanctuary is spread over an area of 120 sq. km and is home to rare endemic species of squirrel, Indian Giant Squirrel. In addition to this, the sanctuary is rich in diverse forms of fauna and flora and is considered one of the biodiversity hotspots of the world. The region is also home to 14 scared groves which are believed to be thousands of years old and the parent trees of several other floral species found in the area. The lush green rainforests of the sanctuary will mesmerize you with its scenic beauty and serene ambience will soothe your senses. The dense jungle is popular for fun-filled and adventurous trekking activities. The meandering trek trails provide the trekkers with an opportunity to explore the wildlife in a unique way. There are no restrictions to enter the conservation area, however, permission is needed from the concerned forest department authorities to visit few places after the sunset.
