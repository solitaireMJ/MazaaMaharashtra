Thane Creek
Flamingo
Sanctuary

The Thane Creek Flamingo Sanctuary is a significant avian habitat located in Mumbai, India. Situated along the eastern coastline of the city, this sanctuary is renowned for being a crucial feeding and nesting ground for various species of flamingos, especially the greater flamingo (Phoenicopterus roseus). The sanctuary's geographical location and the presence of mudflats and shallow waters make it an ideal destination for these elegant, long-legged birds during their migratory journey. The sanctuary spans an extensive area and is designated as a protected zone to conserve the fragile ecosystem that supports the diverse birdlife. Apart from flamingos, the sanctuary also provides a haven for numerous other bird species, both resident and migratory, making it a birdwatcher's paradise.
